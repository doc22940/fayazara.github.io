A short story about Me,
Music & Life

Prahlad Prasad, singer, composer and guitarist from Bengaluru. Prahlad shot to fame by his composition Coffee Huduga. Coffee Huduga, the debut video has won the best music video award at Dada Saheb Phalke International Film Festival organized by "Minibox". His music is strongly rooted and it is Indian light Classical Music With Western Blues And Rock, it is presented with a contemporary global sound. Prahlad, the versatile singer and composer has composed songs in Hindi and Kannada languages.

He had collaboration with California violinist and Grammy Award Winner Rocio Marron for his composition "Yenu Vismayo" and this song entered the 57th annual Grammy Awards. Prahlad's all Kannada compositions are written by Prahlad's father and lyricist S.N. Prasad. Prahlad had been A-Grade artist at Radio medium too, his voice has been highly appreciated and followed by the current generation. He has composed and sung for more than 500 jingles and still counting. Prahlad is a gifted child, he grew up with listening light classical and retro music. His musical journey started at the tender age of four and since then his talent has been acknowledged and appreciated throughout his expedition. 'Sangam Kala Group’ a National Talent Hunt Group that encourages young singers, has awarded Prahlad, as the best Regional singer. He has been on the panel of judges at various inter collegiate music festivals held across Bengaluru. Prahlad is the patron of Prahlad Mira Academy of Music and Art, a venture that intents to nurture young talent and spread the joy of music. Prahlad is Alumnus from Karnataka Chitrakala Parishad.

Prahlad Mira Academy for Music and Art

Ours is a humble effort in propagating the culture of music and art. We motivate every enthusiast who wants to excel in the field of music and art to fulfill their dreams. We have trained and committed teachers, with rich experience in their field. We are duty conscious and provide individual attention to each and every student as to enable them to pursue the field of music and art as their career. We take them to the field trips for interaction with the renowned personalities. Arrangements will be made to exhibit the art works of the students to the public. Music students will be provided proper platform to unveil their talents. We also provide professional placements for the students for their further growth. There will not be any evaluation system in our school, however equal opportunities will be given to each and every student to exhibit their talent. We believe in creating a masterpiece rather than mere simulation.

Our Vision

Every aspirant of music and art, are endowed with unique ability and dreams of their pursuit, which needs to be tapped for its full bloom. We do not believe in teaching according to set down standard, structured syllabus in music and art. Because, art and music cannot be taught like in formal education under set down norms. Art and music are absolute based on one’s perception and experiences of life irrespective of their age. We do not alter the innate style of the student, but we try to sculp out a different dimension towards the culmination of their passion.
